Clarity

A calm, minimal daily habit tracker.

The Vision

Clarity is an antidote to the anxiety of modern productivity tools. It avoids streaks, gamification, and notifications. Instead, it focuses on honest self-reflection and long-term consistency. Built "local-first," it ensures your data never leaves your device.

1. Daily Check-in

The primary interface is a distraction-free form designed for end-of-day reflection.

  • Boolean Habits: Simple "done" / "not done" toggles.
  • Joy Tracking: Completed habits can be marked as "joyful," adding qualitative data.
  • Numeric Habits: Track quantifiable goals (e.g. "Water: 5 cups") with custom units.
  • Moments: A tag-based system to capture daily highlights (e.g. "Good meal", "Nature").
  • Reflection: A simple text area for journaling thoughts.

2. History & Visualization

A powerful retrospective view that emphasizes patterns over streaks.

  • Calendar Heatmap: Blends colors based on habit completion (Blue) and joy (Amber).
  • Interactive Filtering: Tap a specific habit to see only its history on the grid.
  • Frequency Analysis: Statistical breakdown of consistency over configurable periods.
  • Day Detail: Slide-over sheet to review past entries without full edit mode.

3. System Configuration

Complete control over the tracking model without losing historical data.

  • Custom Habits: Create boolean or numeric habits with custom units.
  • Archiving: Archive habits to remove them from the daily view while preserving history.
  • Joy Configuration: Set habits to "Joy by default" (e.g. Meditation is always joyful).

4. Data & Settings

Privacy-first design with full control over your information.

  • Privacy First: All data lives in LocalStorage. No servers.
  • Backup & Restore: Full JSON export/import for data portability between devices.
  • Theming: First-class Dark Mode support (Stone/Charcoal palette).
  • Factory Reset: Option to wipe all data and return to a clean slate.

User Journeys

01. The Daily Reflection Happy Path

Open Alex opens Clarity. The date defaults to "Today".
Track Toggles "Exercise" to Done. Toggles "Reading" to Done and taps the flower icon to mark it as Joyful. Increments "Water" to 6 cups.
Capture Selects "Nature" from Moments. Realizes they also cooked a meal, so they tap + New moment, type "Cooking", and add it instantly.
Reflect In the reflection box, Alex types: "Felt more energetic today after the run."
Save Taps "Save". The button transitions to "Day captured", and the app gently redirects to the History view.

02. Reviewing Patterns Analysis

Navigate Sam taps "History". They see the heatmap—some days are blue (productive), some amber (joyful), most a blend.
Filter Sam wonders, "How often have I been running?" They tap "Frequency" to open the stats list, then tap "Running".
Visualize The heatmap updates. Only days with "Running" checked are lit up. Sam sees they run mostly on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
Detail They tap a specific bright cell from last week. A sheet slides up showing that on that day, they also had "Good sleep", revealing a correlation.

03. Adapting the System Config

Intent Jordan is stopping "Coffee" tracking and starting "Meditation".
Archive In Settings > Manage, Jordan finds "Coffee" and taps Archive. It vanishes from the active list but remains in history.
Create Taps + Add habit. Selects "Boolean", names it "Meditation", and sets "Joy by default" to true.
Verify Jordan returns to the Home screen. "Meditation" is now at the top of the list, ready for tonight.

04. Data Safety & Migration Utility

Export On the old phone, Casey goes to "Settings" > "Your data" and taps Export backup. A JSON file is saved.
Transfer Casey sends this file to their new phone.
Import On the new phone, Casey opens Clarity, goes to "Settings", and taps Import from file.
Success The app confirms: "342 entries imported". Casey goes to "History" and sees their entire year's grid instantly populated.
